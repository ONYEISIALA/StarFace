8. Dodgeball Arena

Concept: Players throw balls at each other in an arena. If you’re hit, you’re out.

Goal: Be the last player standing or the team with survivors.

Core Mechanics:

Pick up and throw balls.

Dodge, jump, or hide behind objects.

Power-ups: “triple throw,” “shield,” or “slow-motion dodge.”

9. Zombie Survival

Concept: Some players start as humans, others as zombies. Eliminated humans turn into zombies.

Goal: Humans survive until the timer ends, zombies infect everyone.

Core Mechanics:

Timer counts down.

Zombies get faster with time.

Last human standing = winner.

10. King of the Hill

Concept: A small area (hill) must be controlled.

Goal: Stay on the hill the longest.

Core Mechanics:

Timer counts how long each player stays on the hill.

Push or knock others off.

First to reach X seconds wins.

11. Freeze Tag

Concept: One player is “it” and must tag others. Tagged players are frozen.

Goal:

If “it” freezes everyone, they win.

If time runs out with unfrozen players, others win.

Core Mechanics:

Players can “unfreeze” each other.

Multiple “it” roles for faster rounds.

12. Bomb Pass / Hot Potato

Concept: A bomb is passed around players. When timer hits 0, the holder is eliminated.

Goal: Don’t be holding the bomb when it explodes.

Core Mechanics:

Tap to pass bomb to nearby player.

Timer speeds up over time.

Last player alive wins.

13. Obstacle Course (Wipeout Style)

Concept: Players race through a funny, exaggerated obstacle course.

Goal: First to finish.

Core Mechanics:

Moving platforms, swinging hammers, slime pits.

Respawn at checkpoints.

Great for chaotic fun.

14. Hide and Seek

Concept: One or two players are seekers, others are hiders.

Goal: Hiders survive without being found before timer ends.

Core Mechanics:

Hiders can disguise as objects.

Seekers get hints as time runs out.

Multiplayer chat makes it more fun.

15. Battle Arena (Mini Brawls)

Concept: All players spawn in a small arena with simple weapons.

Goal: Knock out as many players as possible.

Core Mechanics:

Simple attacks: punch, kick, push.

Items appear randomly (hammer, shield).

Scoreboard counts KOs.

16. Escape Room Challenge

Concept: Teams solve puzzles to escape a locked room.

Goal: First team to solve all puzzles and escape wins.

Core Mechanics:

Memory codes, matching symbols, hidden keys.

Each round has a time limit.

Works well for small 2–5 player groups.

17. Musical Chairs (Digital Version)

Concept: Virtual chairs are fewer than players. When music stops, players scramble for a seat.

Goal: Don’t be left standing.

Core Mechanics:

Chairs reduce every round.

Eliminations until 1 winner remains.

Can use fun sound effects for engagement.

18. Trivia Battle

Concept: Players answer quick-fire questions. Wrong answers eliminate you.

Goal: Last player or team standing wins.

Core Mechanics:

Multiple choice or true/false.

Difficulty increases every round.

Power-ups: skip question, freeze others.
