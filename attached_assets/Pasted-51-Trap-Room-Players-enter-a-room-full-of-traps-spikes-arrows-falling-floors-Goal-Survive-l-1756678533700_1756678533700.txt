Players on rafts in the ocean.

Sharks attack at random.

Collect wood to rebuild raft. Last survivor wins.

53. Mirror Run

Players run through a mirrored world.

Sometimes controls flip (left = right).

Winner = first to finish.

54. Rocket Dodge

Missiles rain down.

Players must dodge explosions.

Last one alive wins.

55. Rope Swing Race

Players swing from ropes to cross gaps.

First to finish wins.

56. Sandstorm Survival

Arena fills with moving sandstorms that reduce visibility.

Hide, dodge, or push others out.

Last survivor wins.

57. Magnet Madness

Map has giant magnets pulling and pushing players.

Avoid being dragged into traps.

Winner = last standing.

58. Emoji Quiz

Multiplayer = fastest correct wins.

59. Rocket Soccer

Like football, but with rocket-powered cars/avatars.

Score goals before timer ends.

60. Spider Web Escape

Players stuck in a giant web.

Must untangle (mini puzzles) before spider arrives.

61. Minefield

Map covered in hidden mines.

Step wrong = explosion.

Goal: Find safe path.

62. Wind Blaster

Arena with giant fans blowing players around.

Avoid falling off.

63. Banana Slip Race

64. Meteor Shower

Survive longest.

65. Camouflage

Players hide among AI clones.

66. Boomerang Battle

Each player has a boomerang.

67. Rope Tug Climb

70. Balloon War

71. Sling Shot Arena

Players fling themselves with slingshots.

72. Dice Duel

Players roll dice. Higher roll can attack.

73. Bridge Collapse

Players run across a bridge.

Sections collapse randomly.

74. Sand Timer Race

75. Alien Abduction

Avoid the beams until timer ends.

76. Boom Box Dance

Music plays, and players must copy moves.

77. Ice Slide Race

Slippery slope race.

Players must slide, jump, and dodge.

First to finish wins.

78. Magic Portal

Players teleport randomly through portals.

Must find the real exit.

79. Cannon Chaos

Arena with cannons shooting balls everywhere.

Dodge or use balls to hit others.

80. Color Switch
